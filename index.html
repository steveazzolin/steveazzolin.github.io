Ciao 👋

My name is Steve Azzolin and I'm currently an ELLIS PhD student co-supervised by Andrea Passerini (University of Trento), Bruno Lepri (FBK), and Pietro Liò (University of Cambridge). Starting from 2024, I'm also an organiser of the LoG Conference.

My PhD is focusing on studying how to make machine learning algorithms more reliable.

INTERESTS: Graph Neural Networks; Representation Learning; Explainability

EDUCATION: BSc in Computer Science; MSc in Artificial Intelligence Systems
